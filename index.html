Our Mission

The Quran is the final scripture
for all of humanity

Tibyaan exists to make the words of the Quran accessible, clear, and meaningful to every person on earth — Muslim and non-Muslim alike. Through 140+ translations, AI-powered features, and beautifully designed apps, we bring the final divine message to every screen, every language, every heart.

Why Tibyaan

Built different, for everyone

01

140+ Translations

The Quran in over 50 languages — from Classical Arabic to Swahili — making the final scripture accessible to every human being on earth.

02

AI-Powered Understanding

Word-by-word morphology, grammatical analysis, and AI features that help you understand not just what the Quran says — but how it says it.

03

Tajweed Annotations

Character-level tajweed color mapping for every ayah — helping learners recite with confidence and precision.

04

No Hardware Required

Tibyaan Masjid runs natively on Samsung and LG smart TVs your mosque already owns. No kiosks. No proprietary devices. No vendor lock-in.

05

Sect-Neutral & Inclusive

Designed for all Muslims and open to all people. No scholarly affiliation, no denominational bias — just the words of the Quran, clearly presented.
